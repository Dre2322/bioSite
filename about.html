Biography

Sherrie Gomez is a multifaceted individual whose life is shaped by her diverse interests, experiences, and the profound influence of her grandparents. Growing up, her grandparents played a significant role in her life, instilling in her values that continue to guide her. Sherrie's love for yard work is one way she stays connected to her roots, finding it therapeutic to care for her plants. In her free time, she can often be found cooking or solving crosswords, hobbies that allow her to unwind and stimulate her mind.

Sherrie is enthusiastic about learning everything she can about wine. This interest led her to a job at HEB, where she initially worked to get out of the house but soon found herself thriving as an assistant in the wine department. Her dedication culminated in winning a wine-selling contest, earning her a memorable trip to Spain. This experience fueled her desire to travel more, with dreams of exploring the Mayan ruins and Greece.
